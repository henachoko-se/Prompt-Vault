# 新緑アイコン作成プロンプト

添付キャラクターをそのまま使い、初夏のレトロポップ雑誌風1:1アイコンを生成します。鯉のぼり・ツバメなどの初夏モチーフ、アーチ状の架空誌名、日本語キャッチコピーを自動生成してレイアウトします。

**投稿媒体:** Threads (@henachoko_pm)

---

````
Image_generation_prompt:
style: Dynamic and pop early summer magazine cover style, retro pop illustration.
aspect_ratio: "1:1"
background_bleed_prevention:
  instruction: Completely fill the entire canvas (up to the four corners) with a refreshing blue sky and fresh green foliage gradient.
layout_strict_safe_area:
  instruction: All important elements (text, character, motifs) MUST be perfectly contained within a "narrow circular safe area" in the center. Absolutely DO NOT place any text or motifs near the edges.
main_subject:
  placement: Center of the safe area.
  character_specification: Use the character from the attached image exactly as is, without altering any clothing, pose, or held items. Do not add new items.
decorative_motifs_early_summer:
  instruction: Freely and harmoniously place early summer motifs (e.g., koinobori, swallows) within the safe area.
magazine_style_text_dynamic:
  main_title: In the upper part of the safe area, dynamically generate a short fictional magazine name (1-2 words) in an arch shape. Strictly avoid specific months like "MAY".
  headlines:
    instruction: Generate VERY SHORT Japanese catchphrases (maximum 4 to 8 characters) related to early summer or fresh greenery. Place them vertically in the empty spaces on the left and right within the safe area. Do NOT use fixed text; let the AI generate varied phrases each time.
color_palette:
  overall_tone: Bright, exciting vivid colors evoking early summer.
  restriction: The color "red" MUST NOT be used for text or any major design elements.
````
