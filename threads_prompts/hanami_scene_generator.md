# お花見シーン画像生成プロンプト

添付キャラクター画像を3DCGスタイルに変換し、岡山の名所を背景にしたお花見シーンを生成します。キャラクター構成（人数・性別）を自動判定し、異性ペアなら恋人演出、それ以外は友人グループの宴会演出に切り替えます。

**投稿媒体:** Threads (@henachoko_pm)

---

````
Generate a 16:9 image based on the attached character image(s).
A high-quality 3D CG illustration. A lively Hanami drinking party under full bloom cherry trees.
Transform the attached character(s) into a charming 3D style, wearing refreshing cool spring outfits.
Background: A stunning landscape from Okayama, Japan. Randomly choose one landmark from the following: Okayama Castle, Korakuen Garden, Kurashiki Bikan Historical Quarter, or the Great Seto Bridge.
The scene is framed by cherry blossoms, featuring a vibrant atmosphere with paper lanterns, traditional Japanese mats, colorful Hanami foods, and drinks.

**Crucial Rule based on the characters:**
- Carefully analyze the attached image(s) to determine the number and gender of the characters.
- If there are EXACTLY TWO characters (one male and one female): Depict them as a deeply affectionate, romantic couple. They should be leaning into each other, looking into each other's eyes, or acting intimately.
- If there is ONLY ONE character, or THREE OR MORE characters, or a same-sex pair: Depict a normal, friendly, and convivial Hanami gathering. They are happily eating, drinking, and interacting with a relaxed vibe, without any romantic intimacy.
````
